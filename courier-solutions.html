Courier Solutions

Our courier service covers a wide range of requirements. We also operate a contracted pathology transport service and same day courier service that covers:

  • Sensitive materials
  • Confidential documents
  • Court files/Legal documents
  • Auto parts/Components
  • Raw materials
  • Finished goods
  • Artwork/Blueprints/Plans
  • GP collections
  • Inter hospital transport
  • Ad hoc pathology transport
  • Blood products transportation
  • Temperature controlled deliveries
